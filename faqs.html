FAQ's

1. How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" button and filling out the required information, such as your name, email address, and password.

2. How can I reset my password?

If you’ve forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. You’ll receive an email with instructions to reset it.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and other popular payment options. Check our payment section for more details.

4. How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order in the "My Orders" section of your account.

5. What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be in their original condition with tags attached. For more details, please visit our Return Policy

6. Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

Orders can be changed or canceled within a short time frame after placing them. Please contact customer service as soon as possible for assistance.

7. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping fees and delivery times may vary based on your location.

8. How can I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team through the "Contact Us" section on the app or website, via email, or through our customer support hotline.
